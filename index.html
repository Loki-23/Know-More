wants to know more about iitr clubs

The competitive academic environment and co-curricular activities on campus keep the students on their toes.

But everyone needs some time off, to relax and recharge . Clubs at IIT-R are the best place for this.

Here you will find each and every thing about the IITR clubs

GROUPS

Geek Gazette

Geek Gazette, IIT Roorkee's technical magazine aims to satisfy the geek inside every one of us by providing insightful articles, tricks & latest tech researches. Grab an issue and you'll proudly wanna call yourself an uber geek.

Cinesec

Cinesec is dedicated in capturing imagination imagination on camera and turning them into videos. We publish vibrant categories of videos including short movies, campus news and trendz, interviews ,animations, info-videos ,gags, events coverage and feedbacks

Cognizance

Cognizance is the annual technical festival of Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. Come together to learn. Come together to grow. Come together to know. Come, be cognizant. Welcome to Cognizance IIT-Roorkee.

Watch Out

Watch Out is the official student media body of the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee

choreography and dance section

To show that IIT is not about just geeks and there is more to us than that. We gather the best dancers from the campus and practice until we are ready to rock the stage.

SDSLabs

SDSLabs (Software Development Section) is a student section at IIT Roorkee that promotes various technical activities.

Information management group

IMG (Information Management Group) is a student's group in IIT Roorkee responsible for development and maintainance of internet and intranet applications in the institute, including the website of IIT Roorkee.

Mobile development group

Mobile development group, SDS, IIT Roorkee is an active student group directing its efforts towards creating useful mobile applications and promoting tech based leaning for the same.

Data Science group

A student organization driving innovation via machine learning.

vision and language group

The Vision and Language Group, part of ACM IIT Roorkee Chapter, is a student run group that aims to foster a research-centric Deep Learning Community at IIT Roorkee.

Team Robocon

A student group of IIT Roorkee competing in an international robotic extravaganza, organized by the Asian Broadcasting Unit (Doordarshan in India) in which teams from all over the world participate.

ASME iitr chapter

The ASME Student Chapter at IIT Roorkee is an undergraduate club comprised of students majoring in various fields of Engineering. Our mission is to promote and enhance technical competency and professional well-being of our members through quality programs
